# 🌊 Welcome to Bahari

**Platform Digital Koperasi Desa Pesisir**

Bahari is a cooperative-based coastal commodity aggregation platform. We empower coastal communities by digitally consolidating their local production through village cooperatives (Kopdes), opening direct market access, and building measurable local economic data.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

Many coastal villages have immense economic potential, yet their production is scattered and rarely managed as a collective force. Bahari solves this by focusing on **Pillar 3: Utilizing Village Economic Potential**.

Our mission is to help coastal village cooperatives transform scattered local production into ready-to-sell collective commodities. Through Bahari:
- **Local Producers** gain better bargaining power.
- **Buyers** receive transparent and high-quality supplies.
- **Villages** build measurable, data-driven coastal economies.

From fishermen, seaweed farmers, and salt makers to coastal MSMEs and community recycling groups, Bahari is designed to be an inclusive ecosystem for all coastal economic drivers.

## 🚀 The Platform Ecosystem

Bahari is built upon three core pillars:
1. **Kopdes Commodity Hub**: The operational center for cooperatives to map, manage, and track digital inventories of local coastal commodities.
2. **Coastal Commodity Marketplace**: A B2B-focused ordering platform connecting verified coastal commodities directly to buyers (restaurants, hotels, retailers, and distributors).
3. **Impact & Transparency Dashboard**: Real-time analytics showcasing economic transactions, active producers, community contributions, and environmental incentives (Eco Points).
